Free time

Scout

I spend a lot of time as a scout leader (volunteer, as everyone is Italy) for the local group of AGESCI.

I joined the Scout Movement as a teenager and I’ve been a scout leader since end of 2015.

I think the ideas of Baden Powell (as they are implemented in Italy, at least) are an effective way to train boys and girls in many aspects of life.

No one can pass through life, any more than he can pass through a bit of country, without leaving tracks behind, and those tracks may often be helpful to those coming after him in finding their way. (B.P., 1940)
